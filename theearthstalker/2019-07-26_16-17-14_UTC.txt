Gili Islands are a group of three islands - Gili Trawangan, Gili Meno and Gili Air in Lombok, Indonesia. What makes Gili Islands unique is that there are no motorcycles and no cars; and the only mode of transport in these beautiful islands are bicycles and horse carriages.
