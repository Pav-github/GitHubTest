The Sigiriya Rock Fortress, is a UNESCO world heritage site that in Sri Lanka, known for its stunning views and incredible history.

To enter the fortress, you need to pay a small entrance fee of USD 30 per person.

This fortress was built by King Kasyapa in the 5th century and is one of the most well-preserved examples of ancient urban planning in the world. From the frescoes on the walls to the stunning water gardens, there’s so much to see here.
