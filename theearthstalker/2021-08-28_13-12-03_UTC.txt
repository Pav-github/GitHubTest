INDIA to MALDIVES ?? Few important things you should know!

✈️ Carry RT-PCR negative report less than 96 hours old
✈️ Fill the travel health declaration form online (Imuga), 24 hours before arrival in Maldives 
✈️ Try to book only 1 hotel (Currently, split stays require special permissions)
✈️ All water sports are permitted
✈️ Choose an all-inclusive / full-board package to save money
✈️ Pick a hotel with RT-PCR testing facilities (Report to be presented on landing in India)

Travel Responsibly and Enjoyy! Cheers!! 🍻
