Galle Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage site located in Sri Lanka, originally built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and later fortified by the Dutch. Today, it’s a vibrant mix of historical architecture, trendy cafes, boutique shops, and stunning ocean views. Whether you’re strolling along the ramparts, admiring the lighthouse, or sipping a latte at a chic cafe, Galle Fort is sure to captivate you with its unique blend of old-world charm and modern flair.
